About Us

Project Statement

Tune In - the music app that connects you with your favorite tunes! Say goodbye to generic music recommendations and hello to a personalized music experience. With Tune In, you can discover a brand new world of music through your friends! Whether you're a fan of rock, pop, hip hop, or any other genre, Tune In has everything you need to stay up-to-date on the latest releases and remain connected with your friends. Download Tune In today and enjoy Tuning In!
